Machine Learning Analytics

Machine learning is a field of computer science that uses statistical techniques to learn from data. It is a subset of artificial intelligence (AI) that is concerned with the development of computer systems able to perform a wide range of tasks, without being explicitly programmed. Machine learning focuses on the development of computer programs that can access data and use statistical algorithms to make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed.
